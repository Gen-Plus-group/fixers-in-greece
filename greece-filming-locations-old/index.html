Top Greece Filming Locations

From ancient ruins to crystal-clear waters, Greece offers unparalleled diversity for your production needs.

🏝️

Santorini

Iconic island with white-washed buildings, blue-domed churches, and stunning caldera views. Perfect for romantic films and luxury commercials.

📍 Cyclades Islands
🎬 Featured in: The Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants
⭐ Best for: Romance, Luxury
🏛️

Athens

Greece's capital combining ancient monuments like the Acropolis with modern urban landscapes. Ideal for historical epics and contemporary dramas.

📍 Attica Region
🎬 Featured in: Troy, 300
⭐ Best for: Historical, Urban
🏰

Thessaloniki

Greece's cultural capital with Byzantine walls, Ottoman architecture, and vibrant waterfront. Perfect for historical dramas and cultural documentaries.

📍 Macedonia, Northern Greece
🎬 Featured in: The Promise
⭐ Best for: Historical, Cultural
🏰

Rhodes Old Town

UNESCO World Heritage medieval city with Knights' castles, cobblestone streets, and Byzantine churches. Magical for period dramas.

📍 Dodecanese Islands
🎬 Featured in: The Guns of Navarone
⭐ Best for: Medieval, Heritage
⛱️

Mykonos

Cosmopolitan island paradise with pristine beaches, windmills, and vibrant nightlife. Perfect for fashion shoots and lifestyle commercials.

📍 Cyclades Islands
🎬 Featured in: The Bourne Identity
⭐ Best for: Lifestyle, Fashion
⛰️

Meteora

Spectacular monasteries perched on towering rock pillars. UNESCO site offering breathtaking aerial shots and spiritual atmosphere.

📍 Thessaly Region
🎬 Featured in: For Your Eyes Only
⭐ Best for: Spiritual, Epic
