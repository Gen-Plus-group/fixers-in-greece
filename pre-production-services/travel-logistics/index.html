How quickly can you process visas and travel documentation?

Standard visa processing takes 5-10 business days for most countries, 3-5 days for expedited services. Emergency visa processing available within 24-48 hours for urgent requirements. Work permit processing varies by country, typically 10-21 days. Rush services available with premium pricing. We maintain relationships with consulates and embassies for priority processing when needed.

Do you handle equipment shipping and customs clearance?

Complete equipment logistics including international shipping, customs clearance, and carnet coordination. Specialized handling for sensitive equipment, comprehensive insurance coverage, and tracking systems. Temporary import permits, duty management, and compliance with local regulations. Emergency shipping services and expedited customs processing for urgent equipment requirements.

Can you coordinate travel for large international crews?

Specialized coordination for large crew movements including group bookings, charter arrangements, and multi-destination itineraries. Crew manifest management, immigration coordination, and group visa processing. Accommodation block bookings, ground transportation fleets, and centralized travel management. Experience with crews of 50+ people across multiple countries and time zones.

What are the typical costs for travel logistics services?

Service fees typically 5-10% of total travel costs for comprehensive coordination. Flat rates for visa processing ($200-500 per person), equipment shipping (varies by weight/destination), and ground transportation. Volume discounts for large crews and long-term projects. Emergency services carry premium pricing. Transparent pricing with detailed breakdowns and no hidden fees.

Do you provide emergency travel support and crisis management?

24/7 emergency travel support including flight changes, accommodation adjustments, and crisis management. Medical emergency coordination, evacuation planning, and emergency repatriation services.
