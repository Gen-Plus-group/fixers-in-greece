Pre-Production FAQs

What's included in pre-production services?

Comprehensive planning including scriptwriting, casting, location scouting, budgeting, scheduling, crew hiring, and all legal documentation needed for production.

How long does pre-production take?

Timeline varies by project scope, typically 4-12 weeks for feature films, 2-6 weeks for commercials, and 1-3 weeks for smaller projects.

Do you work internationally?

Yes, we provide pre-production services worldwide with local expertise and international quality standards in 100+ countries.

Can you handle specific budget requirements?

Absolutely. We work with all budget levels from independent films to major studio productions, providing scalable solutions that fit your financial parameters.

What makes your pre-production services different?

Our integrated approach combines creative excellence with strategic planning, backed by a global network of vetted professionals and proven project management methodologies.
