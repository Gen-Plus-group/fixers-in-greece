Location Scouting FAQ

Essential information for planning your location scouting project in Greece.

How long does location scouting take in Greece?

Scouting timelines depend on project scope and location requirements. Basic commercial locations: 2-3 days. Feature film comprehensive scouting: 1-2 weeks. Documentary location research: 3-7 days. Emergency location sourcing: 24-48 hours. We provide detailed scouting schedules including travel time, site visits, and documentation preparation. Rush scouting available with premium pricing for urgent production needs.

What permits are required for filming at Greeceese locations?

Permit requirements vary by location type and production scale. Public locations require government filming permits. Private properties need owner releases and agreements. UNESCO World Heritage sites require special cultural permits. Commercial filming needs business licenses and tax documentation. We handle all permit acquisition including government liaison, documentation preparation, and compliance verification ensuring legal filming rights.

What are typical location scouting costs in Greece?

Location scouting fees depend on scope and duration. Basic scouting: $500-1,500 USD per day. Comprehensive location packages: $2,000-8,000 USD. Remote location scouting: $1,000-3,000 USD per day. Technical surveys and documentation: $300-800 USD per location. Permit acquisition: $200-2,000 USD depending on location type. Travel and accommodation costs additional. Package deals available for multiple locations.

Can you scout locations in remote areas of Greece?

Yes, we specialize in remote location scouting across all 63 provinces of Greece. Our scouts have access to mountain regions, remote islands, jungle areas, and rural villages. We provide detailed accessibility reports, equipment transport options, and crew accommodation solutions. Safety assessments, emergency evacuation plans, and local support coordination included for challenging remote locations.

How do you ensure cultural sensitivity when scouting heritage sites?

We work closely with cultural authorities, local communities, and heritage site managers to ensure respectful filming practices. Our team includes cultural consultants who understand religious protocols, traditional customs, and community sensitivities. We provide cultural briefings, protocol guidance, and community liaison services ensuring authentic and respectful representation of Greeceese heritage and traditions.

What technical information do you provide in location reports?

Comprehensive technical documentation including GPS coordinates, accessibility maps, power availability, equipment access routes, and parking facilities. Weather patterns, seasonal considerations, and optimal shooting times. Safety assessments, emergency procedures, and crew facility options. High-resolution photography, 360-degree documentation, and drone surveys where permitted. Detailed cost breakdowns and permit requirements for each location.

Do you provide location management during production?

Yes, we offer complete location management services including on-site coordination, permit compliance, and production support. Our location managers handle daily logistics, community relations, and emergency response. Services include crew facilities setup, equipment coordination, security arrangements, and local authority liaison. 24/7 support available throughout your production schedule.

Can you match locations to specific creative requirements or period settings?

Absolutely. Our scouts specialize in creative matching including period-specific architecture, authentic cultural settings, and unique visual environments. We maintain detailed databases categorized by era, style, and visual characteristics. Services include historical research, architectural authentication, and creative consultation ensuring perfect matches for your artistic vision and production requirements.
