Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in professional storyboarding services?

Complete visual breakdown of your script including detailed frame illustrations, camera angle specifications, movement notation, and timing estimates. Professional storyboards include shot descriptions, technical notes, and production planning elements. Digital delivery with high-resolution images, PDF compilations, and editable source files. Revision rounds included with collaborative feedback integration.

How long does storyboard creation take?

Standard turnaround is 48-72 hours for basic storyboards, 5-7 days for detailed illustrated boards. Complex sequences with special effects or action scenes may require 7-10 days. Rush services available with 24-48 hour delivery for urgent projects. Timeline depends on script length, detail level required, and revision complexity. Pre-visualization and 3D animatics require additional time.

Do you offer different storyboard styles and formats?

Multiple artistic styles available including rough sketches, detailed illustrations, digital art, and photorealistic renderings. Format options include traditional panel layouts, vertical mobile formats, and presentation-ready compilations. 3D pre-visualization, motion studies, and animated storyboards available for complex sequences. Custom style matching to project aesthetic and brand requirements.

Can you work with international productions and remote teams?

Full remote collaboration capabilities with cloud-based sharing, real-time feedback systems, and international time zone coordination. Video conferencing for director consultations, digital approval workflows, and secure file sharing. Multi-language support and cultural adaptation for international projects. Experience with global production workflows and industry-standard software compatibility.

What's the pricing structure for storyboarding services?

Competitive pricing based on project scope, detail level, and timeline requirements. Per-frame pricing for standard boards, package deals for full scripts, and hourly rates for consultation work. Volume discounts for series and multi-episode projects. Transparent pricing with no hidden fees, detailed quotes provided after script review. Payment plans available for larger projects.

Do you provide production support and on-set assistance?

On-set storyboard support available including real-time adjustments, continuity assistance, and shot verification. Digital storyboard access via tablets and mobile devices for production teams. Revision services during production, additional shot creation, and post-production storyboard updates. Integration with production management software and workflow systems.
