Scriptwriting FAQ

Essential information for planning your scriptwriting project in Greece.

What are typical turnaround times for scriptwriting projects?

Turnaround times depend on project scope and complexity. Short commercial scripts: 24-48 hours. Feature film treatments: 1-2 weeks. Full feature screenplays: 4-8 weeks. Documentary scripts and treatments: 1-3 weeks. TV episode scripts: 3-7 days. Rush projects available with 50% premium pricing. We provide detailed timelines during initial consultation including revision rounds and feedback integration periods.

How do you ensure cultural authenticity in Greeceese-themed projects?

Our team includes native Greeceese writers and cultural consultants ensuring authentic representation. We conduct thorough research on historical periods, social customs, and contemporary Greeceese culture. Language authenticity review for dialogue, cultural sensitivity screening, and local expert consultation. We avoid stereotypes and ensure respectful portrayal while maintaining dramatic effectiveness and international appeal for co-productions.

What are your pricing structures for scriptwriting services?

Pricing varies by project type and length. Commercial scripts: $500-2,000 USD. Short film scripts (under 30 minutes): $1,500-5,000 USD. Feature film screenplays: $5,000-25,000 USD. Documentary treatments and scripts: $2,000-8,000 USD. TV episode scripts: $2,000-7,000 USD. Package deals available for series or multiple projects. Rush delivery incurs 50-100% premium. Payment plans available for larger projects.

Can you adapt existing material or work from treatments?

Yes, we specialize in adaptations from novels, short stories, true events, and existing treatments. Rights clearance consultation, source material analysis, and adaptation strategy development. We maintain the essence of original material while optimizing for visual storytelling. Experience with international co-productions requiring cultural adaptation and market-specific modifications. Legal consultation available for rights and adaptation agreements.

How many revision rounds are included in your scriptwriting services?

Standard packages include 3 revision rounds after first draft delivery. Additional revisions available at hourly rates. Major structural changes may require renegotiation. We use collaborative platforms for feedback integration, version control, and real-time comments. Revision scope clearly defined in contracts including dialogue polish, scene restructuring, and character development refinements. Rush revisions available for urgent production schedules.

Do you provide script formatting and industry-standard presentation?

All scripts delivered in industry-standard formatting using Final Draft, WriterDuet, or client-preferred software. Proper scene headings, character names, dialogue formatting, and action lines. Shooting script formatting available including scene numbers, camera directions, and production notes. PDF and editable formats provided. Script breakdown services available for production planning including character lists, location requirements, and prop inventories.

Can you write scripts in multiple languages or provide translation services?

We write original scripts in English and Greeceese with native-level fluency. Translation services available for existing scripts between Greeceese, English, and other major languages. Bilingual script development for international co-productions. Cultural adaptation beyond literal translation ensuring dialogue authenticity and cultural relevance. Subtitle-friendly writing for international distribution and localization requirements.

How do you handle intellectual property and confidentiality?

Comprehensive NDAs signed before project commencement. All intellectual property rights transfer to client upon final payment unless otherwise specified. Secure file handling, encrypted communications, and confidential project management. No script content shared or used for promotional purposes without explicit permission. Legal consultation available for complex IP arrangements and co-development agreements. Professional discretion maintained throughout development process.
