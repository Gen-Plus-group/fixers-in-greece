Frequently Asked Questions

What permits are required for filming in Greece?

Permit requirements depend on location type and production scale. Government filming permits required for public locations, cultural sites, and heritage areas. Private property releases needed for commercial locations. Specialized permits for UNESCO sites, military areas, and restricted zones. Drone filming requires separate aviation permits. International productions need additional documentation including co-production certificates and foreign investment approvals.

How long does permit acquisition take?

Standard permit processing takes 5-10 business days for basic locations, 10-15 days for government sites, and 15-30 days for UNESCO World Heritage locations. Emergency permits available with 24-72 hour processing for urgent requirements. Complex international co-productions may require 30-45 days. We provide expedited services through priority channels and established government relationships.

What are the costs for film permits in Greece?

Permit costs vary by location type and production scale. Basic location permits: $200-1,000 USD. Government building permits: $500-2,500 USD. UNESCO World Heritage sites: $1,000-5,000 USD. Drone filming permits: $300-800 USD. Our service fees: $500-2,000 USD depending on complexity. Package deals available for multiple locations. All costs transparent with detailed quotes provided upfront.

Can you handle permits for sensitive or restricted locations?

Yes, we specialize in permits for sensitive locations including government buildings, military installations, religious sites, and cultural heritage areas. Established relationships with relevant authorities and understanding of security protocols. Cultural sensitivity coordination, community liaison, and proper protocol adherence. Special clearance procedures for international productions and documentary filming Greece.

Do you provide insurance and liability coverage coordination?

What support do you provide during production?

Ongoing permit compliance monitoring, government liaison during filming, and emergency permit modifications. On-site coordination with authorities, permit verification, and compliance documentation.
