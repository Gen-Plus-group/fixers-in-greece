Rotoscoping & Cleanup

Precision rotoscoping and invisible cleanup work that forms the foundation of seamless visual effects.

Professional rotoscoping and cleanup services provide the essential foundation for high-quality visual effects through meticulous frame-by-frame work that isolates elements and removes unwanted artifacts. Our skilled roto artists combine technical precision with artistic understanding to create perfect mattes and clean plates that enable seamless compositing and effects integration. From complex hair and motion blur preservation to invisible wire removal and comprehensive cleanup work, we deliver rotoscoping solutions that maintain the highest standards of accuracy while preserving natural movement and fine detail that brings visual effects to life without revealing the technical craft behind the magic.

Rotoscoping and cleanup encompass detailed technical processes including precision masking, edge refinement, motion tracking, paint work, and artifact removal that require patience, skill, and deep understanding of compositing requirements. Our artists utilize industry-standard software including Silhouette, Nuke, Mocha Pro, and specialized roto tools to create frame-accurate mattes that handle complex challenges such as motion blur, transparency, fine hair detail, and rapid movement. We implement efficient workflows combining manual artistry with smart tools like planar tracking and machine learning assistance to deliver consistent, high-quality results that meet the exacting standards of feature film visual effects while maintaining cost-effective production timelines.

Whether isolating complex character performances for background replacement, removing extensive wire rigs from action sequences, or cleaning up production artifacts for invisible effects, our rotoscoping and cleanup services adapt to any technical challenge and quality requirement. We provide comprehensive roto and prep solutions including articulated mattes, holdout masks, garbage masks, wire and rig removal, marker removal, crew and equipment cleanup, and beauty work that ensure perfect preparation for downstream compositing. Our commitment to technical excellence and quality control ensures that every frame receives the detailed attention necessary to create flawless visual effects that maintain complete photographic integrity throughout the most demanding shots.
