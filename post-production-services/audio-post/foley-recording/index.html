Foley Recording

Precision foley artistry that brings authentic human presence and tactile realism to every scene through masterful sound performance.

Professional foley recording services create the essential human elements that make visual storytelling believable through meticulously performed and synchronized sound effects. Our expert foley artists combine physical performance skills with acute auditory perception to reproduce every footstep, clothing rustle, prop interaction, and subtle human movement that audiences expect to hear. From delicate finger taps on surfaces to complex action sequences requiring precise choreography, we deliver foley recordings that seamlessly integrate with production audio while adding the crucial layer of sonic detail that transforms good productions into immersive experiences that feel completely authentic and engaging.

Foley recording encompasses comprehensive performance techniques including footstep articulation across diverse surfaces, cloth and fabric movement simulation, prop handling and interaction sounds, body movements and contact noises, and environmental interaction effects. Our foley stages feature extensive surface collections, prop libraries, and specialized recording equipment including Neumann, Schoeps, and DPA microphones positioned to capture every nuance of performed sound. We utilize Pro Tools HDX systems with video playback synchronization to ensure frame-accurate timing while our artists develop character-specific movement signatures that maintain consistency throughout productions and create subtle audio storytelling that supports character development and narrative progression.

Whether recording intimate character moments for independent dramas, choreographing complex action sequences for blockbuster features, or creating unique movement signatures for animated characters, our foley recording services adapt to any production scale and creative requirement. We provide complete foley solutions including spot effects recording, movement passes, prop work, specialized effects creation, and fully edited stems ready for mixing that ensure comprehensive coverage of all human-generated sounds. Our commitment to performance authenticity and technical precision ensures that every project receives the artistic attention and professional craftsmanship necessary to create believable sonic environments that support visual storytelling without drawing attention to themselves.
