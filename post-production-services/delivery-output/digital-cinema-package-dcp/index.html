Digital Cinema Package (DCP)

Professional DCP creation ensuring flawless theatrical presentation and worldwide cinema distribution.

Professional Digital Cinema Package (DCP) creation services deliver the industry-standard format for theatrical exhibition, ensuring your film is presented with the highest quality in cinemas worldwide. Our expert DCP technicians combine technical precision with deep understanding of cinema standards to create secure, encrypted packages that meet all specifications for commercial theaters, film festivals, and specialty venues. From independent features requiring festival DCPs to major theatrical releases demanding multi-version packages with subtitles and alternate audio tracks, we deliver DCP solutions that guarantee flawless projection while protecting your content through robust encryption and key management systems.

DCP creation encompasses complex technical processes including image encoding, audio packaging, subtitle integration, encryption, and composition playlist assembly that must conform to strict Digital Cinema Initiatives (DCI) specifications. Our technicians utilize professional DCP creation systems and validation tools to ensure frame-accurate encoding at 2K or 4K resolution, with proper color space conversion, audio channel mapping, and metadata implementation. We perform comprehensive quality control including full playback verification on cinema servers, technical compliance checking, and multi-point validation to guarantee that every DCP plays perfectly in any DCI-compliant projection system, avoiding costly screening failures and ensuring optimal presentation quality.

Whether preparing DCPs for international film festival submission, creating encrypted packages for commercial theatrical release, or developing specialized versions with multiple language options and accessibility features, our DCP services adapt to any distribution requirement and technical specification. We provide complete DCP solutions including source material preparation, format conversion, package creation, encryption and KDM generation, quality control verification, and physical media or electronic delivery options. Our commitment to technical excellence and reliability ensures that every DCP receives meticulous attention to detail necessary for successful theatrical presentation, maintaining the artistic integrity of your production while meeting all technical requirements for global cinema distribution.
