Project Overview

Fixers in Greece delivered comprehensive production services for LIDL's "See you in Athens" campaign, a retail commercial that celebrates the dynamic relationship between the international brand and Athens' vibrant local culture. This project showcased authentic Athenian life while highlighting LIDL's integration into the daily lives of Greek families.

Our production team coordinated complex urban shoots across multiple Athens neighborhoods, capturing the authentic energy of the city while managing the logistical challenges of filming in busy commercial areas. The campaign required deep understanding of local culture to authentically represent LIDL's connection to Greek communities.

Working in close collaboration with LIDL's marketing team, we created compelling visuals that demonstrate how the brand has become an integral part of Athens life, featuring real locations, genuine interactions, and the warm spirit that defines both LIDL and the Greek capital.
