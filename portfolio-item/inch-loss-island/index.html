Project Overview

Fixers in Greece provided comprehensive production support for Inch Loss Island, a transformative reality TV series that combines weight loss journeys with the stunning beauty of Greek island locations. Our team facilitated all aspects of the production, from location scouting to logistics management.

The production required careful coordination of fitness facilities, accommodation for participants and crew, and access to various scenic locations across the Greek islands. Our expertise in reality TV production ensured smooth operations throughout the intensive filming schedule.

Working with international production standards, we delivered a seamless filming experience that captured both the personal transformation stories and the inspiring Greek landscape that served as the backdrop for this life-changing journey.
