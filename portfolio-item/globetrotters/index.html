Project Overview

Fixers in Greece provided comprehensive production support for Globetrotters, an international travel adventure series showcasing Greece's incredible diversity. From the Acropolis to remote island villages, our team facilitated access to Greece's most captivating locations.

The production required extensive logistics coordination across multiple regions, including permits for archaeological sites, adventure activity coordination, and cultural immersion experiences. Our local expertise ensured authentic encounters with Greek culture, cuisine, and traditions.

Working with the show's adventurous format, we arranged everything from cliff diving in Zakynthos to monastery visits in Meteora, creating a comprehensive portrait of Greece that goes beyond typical tourist destinations to reveal the country's hidden treasures.
