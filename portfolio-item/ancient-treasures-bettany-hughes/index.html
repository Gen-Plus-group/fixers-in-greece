Project Overview

Fixers in Greece provided specialized production support for this archaeological documentary series, managing permissions for active excavation sites and coordinating with archaeological teams across Greece.

Our expertise in handling sensitive archaeological locations ensured the production could capture exclusive footage while respecting preservation protocols. We facilitated interviews with leading archaeologists and arranged special access to recent discoveries.

The series showcases Greece's ongoing contribution to our understanding of ancient history, featuring groundbreaking discoveries and cutting-edge archaeological techniques.
