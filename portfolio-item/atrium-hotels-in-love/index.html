Project Overview

Fixers in Greece delivered comprehensive production services for Atrium Hotels & Resorts' "In Love With Our Guests" campaign, creating a heartfelt hospitality commercial that captures the essence of Greek philoxenia (love of strangers) and the brand's commitment to exceptional guest experiences. This multi-property production showcases the diverse offerings across Atrium's premium hotel portfolio.

Our production team coordinated complex multi-location shoots across several Atrium properties, managing the intricate logistics of filming in active hospitality environments while maintaining the highest standards of guest privacy and operational continuity. The project required expertise in hospitality marketing and an understanding of authentic Greek service culture.

Working in close partnership with Atrium's brand team, we created emotionally resonant content that authentically communicates the hotel chain's dedication to guest satisfaction, featuring real staff interactions, genuine moments of hospitality, and the distinctive Greek warmth that sets Atrium apart in the competitive hospitality market.
