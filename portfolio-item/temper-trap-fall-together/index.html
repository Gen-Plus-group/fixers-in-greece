About The Project

The Temper Trap's "Fall Together" music video was an ambitious production that merged the band's signature atmospheric indie rock sound with the timeless beauty of Greece's ancient landscapes.

Filming took place across multiple iconic locations, including the ancient ruins of Delphi - once considered the center of the world in Greek mythology - and the dramatic mountain landscapes surrounding Athens. The production captured the mystical quality of these historical sites, creating a visual narrative that perfectly complemented the song's emotional depth.

Our team coordinated complex shoots at protected archaeological sites, working closely with Greek authorities to ensure respectful filming while capturing the awe-inspiring scale and spiritual significance of these ancient places.
