Project Overview

Fixers in Greece provided comprehensive production services for Jet2holidays' "Get Away to Greece" tourism campaign, designed to inspire travelers to discover the beauty and culture of Greece. This commercial was filmed across multiple iconic Greek destinations, capturing the essence of the perfect Greek getaway.

Our team coordinated complex multi-location shoots across the Greek islands and mainland, managing logistics for talent, crew, and equipment transportation. The project required expertise in tourism marketing, showcasing authentic Greek experiences while maintaining the high production values expected for international advertising campaigns.

Working closely with Jet2holidays' marketing team, we delivered stunning visuals that effectively communicate the unique appeal of Greece as a holiday destination, featuring crystal-clear waters, historic architecture, delicious cuisine, and warm Greek hospitality.
