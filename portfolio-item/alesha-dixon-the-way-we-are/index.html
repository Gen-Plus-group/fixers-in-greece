About The Project

Alesha Dixon's "The Way We Are" was an ambitious production that showcased both the contemporary energy of modern Greece and its timeless beauty. The music video combined high-energy choreography with diverse locations that captured the essence of Greek culture.

Our production spanned multiple locations across Greece, from the bustling streets of Athens to serene island beaches, from traditional villages to modern urban landscapes. The video featured large-scale dance sequences with local performers, creating a celebration of unity and diversity that perfectly matched the song's empowering message.

Working with international choreographers and a large cast of dancers, we coordinated complex performance sequences while showcasing Greece's stunning variety of backdrops, creating a visual feast that enhanced Alesha's powerful vocals and message.
