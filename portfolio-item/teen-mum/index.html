Project Overview

Fixers in Greece provided sensitive and professional production support for Teen Mum, a documentary reality series that follows the lives of teenage mothers across Greece. Our team ensured respectful and ethical filming practices while capturing authentic stories.

The production required careful coordination with families, educational institutions, and healthcare providers. Our local expertise and cultural understanding were crucial in building trust with participants and accessing intimate moments in their daily lives.

Working with international documentary standards, we facilitated a production that balanced compelling storytelling with the welfare of the young participants, creating a series that offers genuine insight into the realities of teenage parenthood in modern Greece.
