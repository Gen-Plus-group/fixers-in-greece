Project Overview

Fixers in Greece partnered with SandStone Global to deliver exceptional production services for this landmark historical documentary series. Our team coordinated complex island-hopping logistics, securing permissions for archaeological sites and facilitating expert interviews.

The production spanned multiple Greek islands, each presenting unique challenges and opportunities. Our local knowledge and established relationships ensured exclusive access to restricted areas and helped capture the authentic spirit of island life.

The series has been praised for its insightful exploration of Greek history and culture, with stunning cinematography showcasing the natural beauty and archaeological wonders of the islands.
