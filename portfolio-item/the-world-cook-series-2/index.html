Project Overview

Fixers in Greece provided comprehensive production services for The World Cook Series 2, an international cooking competition that brought together culinary masters from around the globe to compete in Greece. Our team facilitated access to local markets, traditional producers, and stunning cooking locations.

The production showcased Greece's rich culinary heritage, from ancient olive groves to modern Athens restaurants. We coordinated with local food producers, arranged cooking challenges in historic settings, and ensured the competition highlighted the best of Greek gastronomy while meeting international television standards.

Working with world-class chefs and production crews, we delivered a seamless filming experience that captured the intensity of competition alongside the beauty of Greek cuisine, creating compelling television that celebrated both culinary excellence and Greek culture.
