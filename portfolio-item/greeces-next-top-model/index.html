Project Overview

Fixers in Greece provided comprehensive production support for Greece's Next Top Model, the country's premier fashion competition series. Our team coordinated complex fashion shoots across Greece's most photogenic locations, from ancient ruins to modern cityscapes.

The production demanded expertise in fashion industry logistics, including coordination with top photographers, stylists, and designers. We managed everything from runway construction in historic venues to securing permits for shoots at archaeological sites, ensuring each episode showcased both fashion and Greek culture.

Working with international fashion standards, we facilitated a production that discovered new modeling talent while highlighting Greece as a dynamic fashion destination, complete with dramatic eliminations, makeovers, and the glamorous world of high fashion.
