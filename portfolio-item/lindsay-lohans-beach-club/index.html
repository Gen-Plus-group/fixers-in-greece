Project Overview

Fixers in Greece served as the primary production partner for MTV's "Lindsay Lohan's Beach Club," providing comprehensive support for this high-profile celebrity reality series. Set in the glamorous party island of Mykonos, the show followed Lindsay Lohan as she managed her exclusive beach club venue.

Our team coordinated all aspects of the Mykonos production, from securing the beach club location and managing celebrity logistics to handling the complex requirements of filming in one of Greece's most exclusive destinations. The production demanded seamless coordination between international crews, local authorities, and the luxury hospitality sector.

Working with MTV's production standards, we ensured smooth operations throughout the season, capturing the drama, luxury lifestyle, and stunning Mykonos scenery that made the show a compelling viewing experience for international audiences.
