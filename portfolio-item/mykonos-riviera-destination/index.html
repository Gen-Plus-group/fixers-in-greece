Project Overview

Fixers in Greece delivered comprehensive destination production services for Mykonos Riviera's "Find Your Perfect Destination" campaign, creating an inspiring commercial that captures the essence of what makes Mykonos the perfect island getaway. This project showcases the unique blend of cosmopolitan luxury, authentic Greek culture, and natural beauty that defines the Mykonos experience.

Our production team coordinated extensive location shoots across Mykonos' most iconic and hidden gem locations, managing the complex logistics of island filming while capturing both the glamorous resort amenities and the authentic local culture. The project required expertise in destination marketing and an understanding of what drives travelers to choose their perfect vacation spot.

Working in close collaboration with Mykonos Riviera's marketing team, we created compelling visual narratives that demonstrate why Mykonos stands out as the perfect destination, featuring stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, traditional villages, and the exclusive resort amenities that create the perfect balance of excitement and relaxation.
