Project Overview

Fixers in Greece provided comprehensive production services for NIKE's powerful "Dream Crazier" campaign, featuring Ukrainian tennis champion Elina Svitolina. This emotionally charged commercial was filmed across multiple locations in Athens, capturing both the athletic prowess and personal journey of a mother returning to professional sports.

Our team coordinated complex logistics including securing permits for iconic Athens locations, managing a crew of over 50 professionals, and ensuring seamless production despite challenging weather conditions. The project required specialized equipment for high-speed tennis action shots and intimate documentary-style moments.

Working closely with Wieden+Kennedy's creative team and Conscious Minds production, we delivered a visually stunning commercial that resonates with audiences worldwide, showcasing Greece's versatile filming locations from modern sports facilities to atmospheric urban landscapes.
