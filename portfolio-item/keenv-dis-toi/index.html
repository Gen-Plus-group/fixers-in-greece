About The Project

This vibrant music video for French pop sensation Keen'V was filmed across multiple stunning locations in Greece, capturing the essence of summer and the joie de vivre that defines the artist's upbeat style.

The production showcased Greece's diverse landscapes, from pristine beaches with crystal-clear waters to charming traditional villages with their iconic white-washed buildings and blue-domed churches. Our team coordinated complex logistics across multiple islands to capture the perfect blend of natural beauty and authentic Greek culture.

Working with international crews and local talent, we delivered a high-energy production that perfectly complemented the song's uplifting message and Keen'V's dynamic performance style.
