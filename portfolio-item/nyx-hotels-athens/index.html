Project Overview

Fixers in Greece provided comprehensive production services for NYX Hotels' brand film, creating a dynamic lifestyle commercial that captures the modern urban energy and distinctive boutique hospitality experience of NYX Hotels Athens. This project showcases the hotel's contemporary design, vibrant social spaces, and connection to Athens' cultural pulse.

Our production team coordinated sophisticated urban shoots throughout the property and surrounding Athens neighborhoods, managing the complex logistics of filming in active hospitality environments while maintaining the hotel's operational flow. The project required expertise in lifestyle cinematography and an understanding of modern boutique hotel marketing.

Working closely with NYX Hotels' brand team, we created visually striking content that communicates the hotel's unique positioning as a lifestyle destination, featuring contemporary guest experiences, innovative design elements, and the dynamic atmosphere that makes NYX Hotels a hub for modern travelers seeking authentic urban experiences.
