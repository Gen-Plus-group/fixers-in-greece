Commercial Production FAQ

Common questions about commercial video production services in Greece.

What commercial video formats do you produce?

We produce comprehensive commercial content including TV commercials (15s, 30s, 60s), digital advertising videos, social media content, brand videos, product demonstrations, corporate videos, and promotional materials. All content is delivered in multiple formats optimized for television broadcast, online platforms, social media, and mobile viewing with appropriate aspect ratios and resolutions.

How do you handle international brand standards?

Our team has extensive experience working with global brands and advertising agencies. We maintain strict adherence to brand guidelines, color profiles, logo usage, and messaging consistency. We provide detailed pre-production approvals, real-time collaboration tools, and comprehensive quality control processes to ensure all content meets international broadcast and digital platform standards while resonating with Greeceese audiences.

What is the typical production timeline for commercials?

Commercial production timelines vary based on complexity. Simple product demonstrations require 2-3 weeks from concept to delivery. Standard TV commercials typically take 4-6 weeks including pre-production, filming, and post-production. Complex campaigns with multiple locations, celebrity talent, or extensive VFX may require 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed production schedules and maintain regular communication throughout the process.

Do you provide talent casting and celebrity endorsements?

Yes, we offer comprehensive casting services including Greeceese celebrities, professional models, actors, and authentic local talent. Our casting network includes A-list Greeceese entertainers, social media influencers, and everyday people for authentic brand representation. We handle all negotiations, contracts, and talent management ensuring proper usage rights for commercial distribution across all platforms and territories.

What technical specifications do you deliver?

We deliver commercial content in all required technical specifications including 4K/8K Ultra HD, HDR10+, Dolby Atmos audio, and various frame rates (24p, 25p, 30p, 50p, 60p). Our output formats include ProRes, DNxHD, H.264, and broadcast-specific codecs. We provide comprehensive delivery packages with multiple versions, closed captions, separate audio tracks, and detailed technical reports for seamless integration into broadcast and digital workflows.

How do you ensure cultural authenticity in commercial content?

Our Greeceese production team provides genuine cultural insights ensuring authentic representation while meeting international brand objectives. We offer cultural consultation, appropriate festival timing, regional dialect considerations, and lifestyle accuracy. Our local expertise helps brands connect meaningfully with Greeceese consumers while maintaining global campaign consistency and avoiding cultural missteps that could impact brand perception.

What are your commercial production rates?

Commercial production rates vary significantly based on scope, duration, locations, talent, and technical requirements. Simple product videos start from $15,000-25,000 USD. Standard TV commercials range from $50,000-150,000 USD. Complex celebrity campaigns with multiple locations can range from $200,000-500,000 USD. We provide detailed quotes based on specific requirements and offer package deals for multi-commercial campaigns with competitive regional pricing.

Do you offer multi-language versions and subtitling?

Yes, we provide comprehensive multi-language support including Greeceese voiceover, English dubbing, and subtitling in multiple languages. Our services include professional translation, cultural adaptation, lip-sync dubbing, and closed captioning for accessibility compliance. We can deliver versions optimized for different regional markets across Southeast Asia with appropriate cultural modifications and local market considerations.
