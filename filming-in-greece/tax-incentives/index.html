Cash Rebate Greece (CRGR) Program

Program Overview

The Cash Rebate Greece (CRGR) Program was established with Law No. 5105/2024 «Creative Greece», providing a 40% cash rebate on eligible expenses for international productions filming in Greece. This competitive incentive is designed to attract high-quality film, television, and digital content productions to Greece's diverse locations.

  • 40% cash rebate on qualifying Greek expenses
  • Cap rebate amount at €8 million per production
  • Payment after 6 months of production completion
  • Can be combined with 30% tax credit for total savings up to 60%

Eligible Productions

  • Feature films (fiction and documentary)
  • TV movies and series
  • Mini-series
  • Animated films and series
  • Digital games
  • International co-productions

Non-Eligible Productions

  • Commercials and advertisements
  • Reality TV shows
  • TV game shows
  • Sports broadcasts
  • Current affairs programs
  • News programs

Minimum Spending Requirements (2024)

Film and TV Productions:

  • Feature films (fiction): €200,000 minimum
  • Feature films (documentary): €60,000 minimum
  • Short films: €45,000 minimum
  • Mini-series (fiction): €120,000 per episode
  • TV series (fiction): €35,000 per episode
  • TV series (documentary): €25,000 per episode

Animation Productions:

  • Animated films/TV movies: €80,000 minimum
  • Animated short films: €50,000 minimum
  • Animated series: €50,000 per episode

Digital Games:

  • Video game development: €50,000 minimum

Eligible Expenses

Above-the-Line

  • Producer fees
  • Director fees
  • Principal cast
  • Screenwriter fees

Below-the-Line

  • Local crew wages
  • Equipment rental
  • Location fees
  • Transportation
  • Accommodation
  • Catering
  • Post-production in Greece

Additional 30% Tax Credit

In addition to the 40% cash rebate, Greece offers a 30% tax credit that can be deducted from net taxable income. This incentive can be combined with the cash rebate for maximum savings.

Combined Benefits:

  • Standard productions: Up to 50% total benefit (40% rebate + partial tax credit)
  • EU co-productions: Up to 60% total benefit with enhanced tax credits
  • No double-dipping: Expenses claimed for rebate cannot be claimed for tax credit
  • Strategic planning: Our team helps optimize your incentive strategy

Application Process

1

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I combine the cash rebate with the tax credit?

Yes, the 40% cash rebate can be combined with the 30% tax credit, potentially providing up to 50-60% in total benefits. However, the same expenses cannot be claimed for both incentives.

When will I receive the cash rebate?

The cash rebate is paid after 6 months of submitting the complete final documentation, including audited cost reports.

Is there a cap on the rebate amount?

The total of the rebate amount is disbursed as a one-time payment to the beneficiary production and does not exceed eight million (8.000.000) euros per audiovisual work.

Do I need a Greek production company?

Yes, you need to work with a Greek production services company. Fixers in Greece can serve as your local partner and handle all requirements.
