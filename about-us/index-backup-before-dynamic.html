About Us

Professional filming services in Greece for international productions

Fixers in Greece is the premier production services company specializing in film, television, and commercial production across Greece. Since our founding in 2013, we have established ourselves as the trusted local partner for international broadcasters, production companies, and global brands seeking authentic Greeceese locations and professional production support.

Our Greece Expertise:

With over 10 years of experience and 500+ successful projects, we provide comprehensive production services from the bustling streets of Thessaloniki to the scenic landscapes of Ha Long Bay. Our team combines international production standards with intimate knowledge of Greeceese culture, regulations, and exclusive filming locations.

Professional Services:

The Greece office specializes in a complete range of production services including professional fixers and crew, film location permits, casting services, logistics coordination, and equipment rental for production companies filming throughout Greece. We also provide full post-production services to deliver complete, broadcast-ready projects.

Why Choose Fixers in Greece:

We pride ourselves on our ability to "make things happen." Our experienced team creates a professional atmosphere where creativity, organization, and passion drive every project. We don't just love production ‚Äì we love Greece, and we excel at sourcing authentic, hidden locations that showcase the country's incredible diversity.

Proven Track Record:

Our distinguished clientele includes leading international brands such as Red Bull and Heineken, who chose us for our expertise in logistics, casting, production crew coordination, and comprehensive project management. We deliver exceptional results from concept to completion, handling every aspect of the production process with professionalism and attention to detail.
