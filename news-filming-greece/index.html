News Production FAQ

Common questions about news filming and broadcasting services in Greece.

How quickly can you respond to breaking news?

Our emergency response time is 2 hours for breaking news coverage in Thessaloniki and Athens, with nationwide coverage available within 4-6 hours. We maintain 24/7 standby crews and equipment ready for immediate deployment. Our mobile satellite units can be operational within 30 minutes of arrival on location, providing live broadcast capabilities for urgent news events.

Do you work with international news networks?

Yes, we regularly support major international news organizations including CNN, BBC, Reuters, Associated Press, and other leading networks. We have established relationships with news bureaus and correspondents throughout the region, providing consistent technical support, local knowledge, and cultural guidance for accurate news coverage that meets international broadcasting standards.

What live broadcasting capabilities do you offer?

We provide comprehensive live broadcasting services including HD/4K satellite uplinks, multi-camera live productions, cellular bonding for IP streaming, and backup transmission systems. Our mobile satellite trucks offer Ku-band transmission capabilities, while our 5G/4G bonding systems provide reliable streaming for breaking news. We also offer technical director services and real-time quality monitoring.

Can you provide interpretation and translation services?

Yes, we offer professional interpretation services in Greeceese, English, and other major languages. Our team includes experienced news interpreters familiar with political, economic, and technical terminology. We provide simultaneous interpretation for live broadcasts, consecutive interpretation for interviews, and rapid translation services for written materials, press releases, and official documents.

How do you handle government and official access?

Our experienced team has established relationships with government agencies, official spokespersons, and media liaison offices. We assist with press accreditation, interview arrangements, access to official events, and coordination with security personnel. Our local knowledge ensures proper protocol adherence while maintaining professional journalism standards and respecting cultural sensitivities in official settings.

What are your rates for news production services?

News production rates vary based on urgency, location, crew size, and technical requirements. Standard day rates range from $800-1,500 USD for camera operator and equipment. Breaking news emergency response includes premium rates of 150-200% of standard pricing. Live broadcasting with satellite uplink ranges from $2,000-4,000 USD per day. We offer competitive package rates for multi-day coverage and established news bureau clients.

Do you provide archival footage and research support?

Yes, we maintain extensive archives of Greeceese news footage and provide research support for historical context. Our team can source archival materials, coordinate access to government archives, and provide background research for complex stories. We also offer fact-checking services, source verification, and cultural context to ensure accurate and comprehensive news coverage that meets international journalism standards.

How do you ensure safety during crisis coverage?

Safety is our top priority during crisis and emergency coverage. Our crews receive specialized training in hostile environment awareness, emergency evacuation procedures, and crisis communication protocols. We maintain safety equipment including protective gear, emergency communication devices, and medical supplies. We coordinate with local authorities and emergency services to ensure safe access while maintaining professional coverage standards.
