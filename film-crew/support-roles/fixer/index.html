Professional Fixer Services

Local expertise and operational support ensuring seamless international production logistics and regulatory compliance.

Professional fixer services provide essential local knowledge and operational support that bridges the gap between international productions and local environments, ensuring smooth logistics, regulatory compliance, and cultural navigation throughout the filming process. Our experienced fixers bring deep understanding of local customs, government procedures, business practices, and logistical networks that enable foreign productions to operate efficiently while maintaining positive relationships with local communities, authorities, and service providers. From initial location scouting through final wrap, fixers serve as trusted intermediaries who anticipate challenges, resolve issues quickly, and facilitate seamless production operations that meet international standards while respecting local protocols.

Fixers excel at managing complex coordination between international production teams and local resources, handling everything from permit acquisition and government relations to vendor negotiations and crew coordination with cultural sensitivity and professional expertise. Our fixers understand the nuances of working across different regulatory environments, language barriers, and business practices while maintaining clear communication channels between all stakeholders. They provide strategic guidance on local customs and protocols, facilitate introductions to key contacts, and ensure that productions can focus on creative excellence while having confidence that all operational aspects are being managed by knowledgeable professionals who understand both international production standards and local requirements.

Each fixer in our network combines extensive local knowledge with international production experience, offering bilingual communication skills, established government and vendor relationships, and proven ability to solve problems quickly and efficiently under pressure. They serve as cultural liaisons who help productions avoid common pitfalls while maximizing opportunities, ensuring that international crews can work confidently in new environments while maintaining positive community relations and achieving production goals within budget and schedule constraints, working closely with line producers and production managers. Our fixers pride themselves on being proactive problem-solvers who anticipate needs, provide solutions before issues arise, and maintain the highest standards of professionalism and discretion throughout every phase of production support.
