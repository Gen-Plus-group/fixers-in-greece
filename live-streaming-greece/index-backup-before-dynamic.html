Live Streaming FAQ

Essential information for planning your live streaming production in Greece.

What internet speeds are required for professional live streaming in Greece?

For professional quality streaming, we recommend minimum upload speeds of 10 Mbps for HD (1080p) and 25 Mbps for 4K streaming. Greece's fiber optic infrastructure in major cities supports these requirements. We conduct pre-event internet speed tests, provide backup connections including 4G/5G bonding, and use adaptive bitrate technology to maintain stream quality even with fluctuating connections. Our mobile streaming units include satellite backup capabilities for remote locations.

Can you stream to multiple platforms simultaneously?

Yes, we offer multi-platform streaming to unlimited destinations including YouTube, Facebook, LinkedIn, Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Vimeo, custom RTMP endpoints, and corporate streaming platforms. Each platform can receive optimized streams with platform-specific graphics, resolution, and formatting. We manage platform-specific requirements, handle different aspect ratios, and provide unified analytics across all streaming destinations while maintaining consistent quality and branding.

How do you handle live streaming costs and pricing?

Live streaming costs depend on duration, complexity, and audience size. Basic single-camera streams: $800-1,500 USD for 2-4 hours. Multi-camera productions with graphics: $2,000-4,000 USD. Large-scale events with interactive features: $4,000-8,000 USD. Costs include equipment, crew, streaming infrastructure, bandwidth, and platform setup. Additional charges for custom graphics, simultaneous translation, and extended streaming duration. Volume discounts available for multiple events or ongoing streaming services.

What backup systems do you have for critical live streams?

We implement comprehensive redundancy including backup streaming servers, redundant internet connections, backup power systems, duplicate encoding hardware, and hot-standby equipment. Critical streams use multiple internet providers simultaneously with automatic failover. Our mobile streaming units include satellite backup connections. We maintain backup streaming destinations and can switch between primary and backup systems without interrupting the viewer experience. 24/7 monitoring ensures immediate response to any technical issues.

Can you integrate Greeceese and English commentary for international audiences?

Yes, we provide multi-language streaming solutions including separate audio tracks for Greeceese and English commentary, simultaneous interpretation services, real-time subtitle overlay, and language-specific graphics. Viewers can select their preferred language during the stream. Our team includes bilingual technical operators and interpreters familiar with Greeceese culture and international broadcast standards. We also provide post-event recordings with multiple language options for on-demand viewing.

How do you manage audience interaction during live streams?

We provide comprehensive audience engagement management including moderated live chat, real-time Q&A collection and display, live polling with instant results visualization, virtual hand raising for audience participation, and social media integration. Our dedicated stream moderators filter inappropriate content, highlight important questions, and coordinate with event hosts. We use professional audience engagement platforms that integrate with major streaming services and provide detailed engagement analytics.

What time zones do you accommodate for global live streaming?

We accommodate all global time zones with flexible scheduling for Greece-based productions. Greece time (GMT+7) is strategically positioned for Asia-Pacific audiences but we regularly stream for European and American time zones. Our team provides time zone coordination, multiple broadcast windows for global reach, and recorded playback for different regions. We offer early morning and late-night production services to reach optimal viewing times for international audiences while leveraging Greece's cost advantages.

Do you provide post-stream analytics and recordings?

Yes, we provide comprehensive post-stream analytics including viewer numbers, engagement metrics, geographic distribution, device usage, stream quality statistics, and audience retention data. All streams are automatically recorded in full HD with chapter markers for easy navigation. We deliver edited highlights, individual presentation segments, and raw footage for further use. Analytics reports include recommendations for improving future streams and insights into audience behavior and preferences across different platforms and regions.
