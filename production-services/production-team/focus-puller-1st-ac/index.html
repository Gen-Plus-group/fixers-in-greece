Focus Puller / 1st AC Services Greece

Precision focus control and expert camera assistance for flawless cinematography.

The Focus Puller, or 1st Assistant Camera (1st AC), is a critical member of the camera department responsible for maintaining sharp focus throughout every shot. Our experienced focus pullers in Greece combine technical precision with artistic understanding, ensuring that every frame meets the exact creative and technical requirements of the production. Working with all major camera systems and lens configurations, they deliver consistent excellence across feature films, commercials, television series, and documentaries.

Beyond focus control, our 1st ACs manage comprehensive camera department responsibilities including lens changes, filter management, camera maintenance, and technical troubleshooting. They prepare detailed camera reports, manage digital media, coordinate with the DIT, and ensure smooth workflow between camera and post-production. Their expertise with wireless focus systems, lens mapping, and distance calculation enables them to handle complex shots including Steadicam sequences, crane moves, and challenging low-light conditions.

Our focus pullers are known for their calm professionalism under pressure, whether pulling focus on a critical close-up during an emotional scene or managing split-second adjustments during high-speed action sequences. They work seamlessly with camera operators and DPs to anticipate shot requirements, prepare equipment efficiently, and solve technical challenges that could otherwise delay production.
