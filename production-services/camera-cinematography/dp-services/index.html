Director of Photography Services

Cinematic vision and technical expertise from award-winning cinematographers.

Director of Photography (DP) services represent the creative and technical pinnacle of cinematography, combining artistic vision with cutting-edge technology to capture compelling visual narratives. Our award-winning DPs bring decades of experience across feature films, television productions, commercials, and digital content, working with industry-leading camera systems including RED, Arri Alexa, Sony FX series, and specialized cinema cameras to deliver broadcast-quality results.

Professional DP services encompass comprehensive visual storytelling through expert camera operation, lens selection, lighting design, color workflow management, and technical planning. Our Directors of Photography collaborate closely with directors and producers to establish the visual language of your project, creating custom shooting approaches that enhance narrative impact while maintaining production efficiency and staying within budget constraints.

From intimate interview setups to large-scale multi-camera productions, our DP services adapt to any project scope and creative requirement. We provide complete camera department leadership, including focus pullers, camera operators, and technical support, ensuring seamless integration with lighting, sound, and production teams while delivering cinematic excellence that elevates your content above the competition.

Frequently Asked Questions

What camera systems do your DPs work with?

Our DPs are experienced with all major cinema cameras including RED (Komodo, V-Raptor, Monstro), Arri Alexa (Mini LF, 35, SXT), Sony FX series (FX9, FX6, FX3), and Blackmagic cameras. We select the best system for each project's requirements and budget.

Do you provide camera equipment or just the DP?

We offer both options - DP services only or complete camera packages with experienced operators. Our DPs can work with your existing equipment or we can provide full camera, lens, and support gear packages.

What's included in pre-production consultation?

Our DPs provide creative consultation, technical planning, equipment recommendations, lighting design, shot list development, and workflow planning. This ensures your vision is achievable within budget and timeline constraints.

Can you handle multi-camera productions?

Yes, our DPs are experienced in complex multi-camera setups for live events, concerts, sports, and narrative productions. We coordinate camera positions, matching, sync, and ensure consistent visual quality across all angles.

What's the typical project timeline?

Timeline varies by project scope. Single-day shoots can be arranged with 48-hour notice, while feature films require 2-4 weeks pre-production planning. We accommodate urgent requests and extended productions equally.

Do you provide color workflow support?

Absolutely. Our DPs create custom LUTs, establish color pipelines, and provide post-production collaboration. We ensure consistent color from capture through final delivery, working closely with colorists and editors.

Are your DPs available internationally?

Yes, we have experienced DPs available worldwide. Our network includes local professionals with international experience, ensuring you get world-class cinematography with local knowledge and efficiency.

What's the pricing structure for DP services?

Pricing varies based on project scope, duration, equipment requirements, and location. We offer competitive day rates, weekly rates, and project-based pricing. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your specific needs.
